I am a Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon Web Services (AWS) AI Labs, where I work on Computer Vision and Machine Learning. My research interest broadly lies in using efficient amount of supervision in multimodal data to build autonomus AI agents.

I have done my PhD (ABD) in the Department of Computer Science at University of Toronto, where I worked with Professor Raquel Urtasun and Professor Sanja Fidler. My thesis title was: Efficient Search Strategies in 3D for Visual Scene Understanding. I completed my MS at Toyota Technological Insitute at Chicago (Sept ‘12 - Dec ‘13), under the supervision of Professor Raquel Urtasun.
